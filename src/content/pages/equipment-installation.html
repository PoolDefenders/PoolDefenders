Smart Pool Pump Control with Jandy iQpump

Upgrade your pool with modern, app-based control. The Jandy iQpump allows you to manage your variable-speed pool pump directly from your smartphone—no full automation system required.

With iQpump installed, you can start and stop your pump, adjust RPM speeds, set daily schedules, and monitor energy usage from anywhere. It’s a simple, cost-effective way to add smart control to your pool and improve efficiency.

Heavy-Duty Pool Cleaning with the Polaris Quattro

For pools that deal with heavy debris, the Polaris Quattro pressure-side cleaner delivers powerful, reliable cleaning you can count on. Designed for performance, the Quattro uses a dedicated booster pump to aggressively remove leaves, dirt, sand, and larger debris—without clogging your pool’s filtration system.

Its large wheels and advanced traction allow it to climb walls, navigate tight corners, and clean efficiently across a wide range of pool surfaces. A high-capacity debris canister captures more material in a single cycle, reducing maintenance and improving overall cleaning results.

Cooler, Longer-Lasting Pool Lighting with Jandy HydroCool LED Technology

Modern pool lighting should be as durable as it is beautiful. Jandy HydroCool LED technology is engineered to actively manage heat, keeping internal components cooler for improved reliability and a longer lifespan—especially in hot, sun-exposed environments.

By efficiently dispersing heat away from sensitive electronics, HydroCool helps reduce wear, prevent premature failure, and maintain consistent lighting performance over time. The result is brighter illumination, longer service life, and fewer lighting issues compared to traditional pool lights.
