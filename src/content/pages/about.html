About Pool Defenders

Pool Defenders is owned and operated by Joel Cline, a pool professional with over 20 years of hands-on experience in the swimming pool industry. What began as a family-owned pool company has grown into a career built on technical expertise, honest service, and long-term customer relationships.

Joel got his start at just 18 years old, helping grow a struggling pool company into a trusted name through hard work, strong customer service, and deep industry knowledge. Over the years, he gained extensive experience in pool service, equipment repair and installation, and advanced pool systems—working hands-on with everything from pumps and filters to heaters, automation, and salt systems.

Today, Pool Defenders focuses on reliable pool maintenance, expert equipment repair, and professional installations for residential pools. We service and repair equipment from the industry’s leading brands, including Jandy, Pentair, and Hayward, and take pride in diagnosing problems correctly the first time—saving homeowners time, stress, and unnecessary costs.

At Pool Defenders, our approach is simple: show up on time, communicate clearly, and treat every pool like it’s our own. Whether you need weekly service, a system upgrade, or dependable repairs, our goal is to protect your investment and keep your pool running safely, efficiently, and worry-free.
